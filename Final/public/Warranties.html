Call, email, or visit our Rondebosch centre. Have your purchase receipt and vehicle details ready.

Our technician inspects the tyre to confirm the defect and determine whether it falls under workmanship, manufacturer, or road hazard cover.

03

Valid claims are approved on the spot or escalated to the manufacturer within 2 business days. You'll be kept updated throughout.

04
RESOLUTION

Depending on the outcome, we'll repair, replace, or issue a pro-rated credit — whichever best resolves your situation.

WHAT'S NOT COVERED
  • Damage from incorrect tyre pressure or neglected inflation
  • Wear caused by misaligned wheels or faulty suspension components
  • Damage from racing, off-road abuse, or overloading beyond rated capacity
  • Tyres fitted by a third-party service provider (not Faiq's Tyres)
  • Cosmetic damage such as scuffs or discolouration not affecting performance
  • Damage resulting from a vehicle accident or collision
  • Tyres that have exceeded their tread wear indicator
COMMON QUESTIONS
Do I need my original receipt to claim?
Yes, proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims. If you created an account with us, your purchase history is saved and can be used in lieu of a paper receipt.
How long does the claims process take?
Workmanship claims are typically resolved same-day. Manufacturer defect claims that require the brand's involvement may take 2–5 business days. We'll communicate every step of the way.
Can I claim if I moved to another city?
Yes. Contact us remotely and we'll guide you through the process. For manufacturer defects, we can often arrange assessment through an authorised dealer near you.
Is road hazard cover automatic?
Road hazard cover is an optional add-on, not included by default. It must be elected at the time of purchase. Check your invoice or account purchase history to confirm if you opted in.
What happens if my tyre is only partially worn?
For valid claims on partially-worn tyres, we offer a pro-rated replacement credit based on the remaining tread depth relative to the original tread. This credit is applied toward a replacement tyre.
